Welcome to the **Mobile** section! This is where I explore the world of mobile app development — building smooth, native-feeling experiences that run on both iOS and Android.

## What You'll Find Here {#what-youll-find}

Mobile development comes with its own set of challenges and rewards. Here's what I'll be covering:

- **React Native**: Cross-platform development, navigation, animations, and bridging native modules
- **Expo**: Rapid prototyping, OTA updates, and the managed vs bare workflow tradeoffs
- **Performance**: Jank-free UIs, flatlist optimization, Hermes engine, and profiling tools
- **Native APIs**: Camera, location, push notifications, biometrics, and device storage
- **App Store Deployment**: Signing, build configs, CI/CD pipelines for iOS and Android releases
